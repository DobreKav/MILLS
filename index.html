How to Play

Phase 1 — Placement

Take turns placing your 9 pieces on any empty intersection.

Phase 2 — Movement

Slide a piece to an adjacent empty intersection along a board line.

Phase 3 — Flying

When you have only 3 pieces left, you may move to any empty intersection.

Mills & Captures

Forming 3 in a row (a mill) lets you remove any one opponent piece. Pieces in a mill are protected — unless no other pieces exist.

Winning

Reduce your opponent to 2 pieces, or leave them with no legal moves.
