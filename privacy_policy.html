Privacy Policy

App: Mills — Nine Men's Morris
Package: com.mills.ninemenmorris
Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Mills — Nine Men's Morris collects the following data to provide its online multiplayer feature:

  • Anonymous user ID: A randomly generated identifier assigned by Firebase Authentication. This ID is not linked to your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Google Play Games display name (optional): If you are signed in to Google Play Games, your public gamer display name may be shown to your opponent during online matches.
  • Game move data: Moves made during online matches are temporarily stored in Firebase Realtime Database and deleted when the match ends.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with an online opponent (matchmaking).
  • To synchronize game moves between players in real time.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

3. Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication & Realtime Database
  • Google AdMob — Advertising
  • Google Play Games Services — Optional sign-in

5. Data Retention

Anonymous session data and match data are deleted automatically after each game session. No personal data is stored long-term on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is rated for general audiences.
